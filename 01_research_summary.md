Ryomen Sukuna is a fictional character from the manga and anime series Jujutsu Kaisen created by Gege Akutami. Here are key facts, latest updates, statistics (if available), bullet-point highlights, and source links:

- **Key Facts:**
  - Sukuna is the main antagonist of Jujutsu Kaisen.
  - He was a Heian Era sorcerer.

- **Latest Updates:**
  - There have been discussions about Sukuna's return in the series. Some sources suggest he may have reincarnated, while others indicate that his value as a character has decreased due to significant meta changes.

- **Statistics (if available):**
  - No specific statistics are provided for Sukuna.

- **Bullet-point Highlights:**
  - Sukuna didn't die because of jujutsu; he died when separated from Megumi's body.
  - He is described as a tall, muscular man with four arms, four eyes, and a large mouth on his midriff. His hair is short pink-red, and his eyes are red.
